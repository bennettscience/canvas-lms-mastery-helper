This application is meant to help you gather and sort through Outcome data in Canvas. It is still in testing, so please be patient. Currently, you can do everything except sync grades to Canvas.

Recent updates

  • Grade sync: Post aligned assignments to the Canvas gradebook. If a student has reached the mastery score, they will receive full points for the aligned assignment.
  • Sort roster by name or outcome score.
  • Sync rubric scores nightly for your imported outcomes.

In progress

  • Student dashboard view
  • Keyboard navigation for accessibility.
  • Help tooltips for different actions in the dashboard.

Getting Started

If you don't have any courses in the left sidebar, click Add new course at the top left to import one. Your students are automatically imported.

Open a course by clicking its card on the left. Import Outcomes and Assignments you'd like to track.

Once you have some materials imported, Outcomes watch Assignments and update the score when it's over your Mastery Score. Set each alignment on the Outcome card.

Student attempts are available on each outcome. Click the student's score to see the attempts listed by date. If more that one attempt has been imported, a trendline is also displayed. Outcome attempts are imported for all courses nightly.

Set your mastery score calculation, your mastery score, and manage your imported courses in the Preferences section.
